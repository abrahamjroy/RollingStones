OUR ACTIVITIES

For what we do defines our will to excel.

  • Toastmasters International

    Do you want to become a confident public speaker and strong leader? If so, Toastmasters is the place for you. You'll find a supportive learn-by-doing environment that allows you to achieve your goals at your own pace.

  • Debate Sessions

    Gain broad, multi-faceted knowledge cutting across several disciplines outside the your normal academic subjects.Warning: Increases learners' confidence, poise, and self-esteem. Providing an engaging, active, learner-centered activity.

  • Mock Interviews

    Reduce your interview anxiety by familiarizing you with the situation. Boost your confidence about the things you are doing well, Allowing you to preview any interviewing weaknesses you have.

  • Literary Activities

    Want a session of Pictionary to improve upon your gesturing skills?What about some JAM(Just A Minute) sessions to test your reflexes. We also hold other innovative sessions to bring forth that hidden Nakama in you!

  • Competitions & Events

    We have been known to hold various events, which bring about a huge turnout and an increased awareness of our languages,and awareness of situations,namely, our long running CSI(Crime Scene Investigation) series,Game of GAB,Mr & Ms Nautilus/Bharatham,Poetry Exhibitions,poetry and prose competitions.

  • Interview Prep

    We conduct multiple interview prep activities such as Group Discussion session,Stress Interviews, Literary aptitude portions among others.

Tete-a-Tete
